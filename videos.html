Short videos for social media channels

The following examples showcase various projects I've had the opportunity to work on over the past six years as part of the Global Creative Team at Avon. These projects include social media reels and stories focused on fragrance, skincare, makeup, seasonal campaigns, educational content, and assets aligned with current social trends, such as the Barbie phenomenon. I've also created videos supporting special causes, including Breast Cancer Awareness Month. All of these assets were produced for our global markets and Avon Worldwide social accounts. My primary role involved transforming raw footage into captivating stories that engage audiences and enhance visual storytelling. Additionally, I contributed to storyboarding and content creation, helping to keep production costs down. While the examples below are presented in two formats—9:16 and 1:1—they were optimised for various platforms and size requirements.
